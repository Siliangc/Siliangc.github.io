Starting your adventure

Perhaps you have never heard of the game Spiral Knights, or barely finished the tutorial when you stumbled upon this page. As an introduction, Spiral Knights is a multiplayer team based dungeon game. Despite the closed off dungeon aspect of the game, it maintains an atmosphere which creates a sensation of a open world game. With randomized levels and new parties every trip through this mechanical world, it keeps many players coming back for more. I highly recommend this game for its friendly nature and opportunities for social interaction, along with its late-game share of intensity for the more hardcore of gamers.
